How long does it take to implement an automation solution? +
Most automation projects are completed within 2-4 weeks, depending on complexity. Simple workflows can be live in as little as 3-5 days, while enterprise-level integrations may take 6-8 weeks.
What's the typical ROI of automation? +
Our clients typically see ROI within 3-6 months through time savings, reduced errors, and increased productivity. On average, businesses save 60-80% of time spent on repetitive tasks.
