SAAS • AUTOMATION

Lead Automation System

A rapidly growing SaaS company was struggling to keep up with incoming leads. Their sales team spent hours manually qualifying prospects, following up, and updating their CRM, leading to missed opportunities and slow response times.

The Challenge: Scale lead management without proportionally scaling headcount, while maintaining personalization and response quality.

Our Solution: We designed and implemented an intelligent automation system using n8n that automatically scores leads based on engagement and demographics, triggers personalized email sequences based on behavior, routes high-value prospects to the appropriate sales rep, and maintains perfect synchronization with their CRM.

E-COMMERCE • AI

AI Customer Support System

An e-commerce brand was overwhelmed during peak seasons, with customer support wait times exceeding 24 hours. The high volume of repetitive questions was burning out their support team and hurting customer satisfaction scores.

The Challenge: Provide instant, accurate support at scale while reducing operational costs and maintaining service quality.

Our Solution: We deployed an advanced AI chatbot powered by GPT-4 that handles order tracking, product recommendations, return processing, and common queries autonomously. The system seamlessly escalates complex issues to human agents with full context, ensuring no customer falls through the cracks.

Multi-Platform Workflow Integration

A digital marketing agency was losing billable hours to manual data entry and status updates across their project management, time tracking, invoicing, and client communication platforms. The disconnected systems led to errors and client communication delays.

The Challenge: Unify disparate systems into a cohesive workflow that eliminates manual data entry and ensures real-time accuracy across all platforms.

Our Solution: We built a comprehensive integration hub that automatically syncs project updates, generates invoices from time entries, sends client reports, and maintains a single source of truth across all systems. Custom APIs ensure bulletproof reliability and data integrity.
