Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our automation solutions

How quickly can the automation be set up? +
Most automation setups are completed within 3-5 business days. For Social Media Automation, we typically have you up and running in 3 days. Shopify-WhatsApp Integration takes about 5 days including testing. We provide full training and support throughout the process.
Do I need technical skills to manage the automation? +
Not at all! Our systems are designed to be user-friendly. We provide a simple dashboard where you can monitor conversations, update responses, and view analytics. We also provide comprehensive training and 24/7 support to help you manage everything easily.
Can I customize the automated responses? +
Absolutely! Every response is fully customizable to match your brand voice. You can set up different response templates for different scenarios, add personalization, and even create conditional flows based on customer queries. You have complete control over what your automation says.
What happens if the bot can't answer a question? +
Our smart routing system automatically forwards complex queries to your team. You'll receive instant notifications via WhatsApp, email, or your preferred platform. The bot gracefully hands over the conversation while keeping the customer informed that a human is taking over.
Is my customer data secure? +
Yes! We use bank-level encryption and are fully compliant with GDPR and data protection regulations. All customer data is stored securely, and we never share or sell your information. You maintain complete ownership and control of your data at all times.
What's the pricing structure? +
Pricing depends on your conversation volume and specific needs. We offer flexible monthly plans starting from $199/month for Social Media Automation and $299/month for Shopify-WhatsApp Integration. Contact us for a custom quote based on your business requirements. All plans include setup, training, and ongoing support.
Can I try it before committing? +
Yes! We offer a free consultation and demo where we'll show you exactly how the automation works with your business. We can even set up a trial run with limited functionality so you can see the results firsthand before making a commitment.
Do you provide ongoing support? +
Absolutely! All our plans include 24/7 technical support, regular system updates, and optimization recommendations. We're here to help you succeed and continuously improve your automation performance.
