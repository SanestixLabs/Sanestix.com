Our Story
Why We Started Sanestix

Saad and Wahab watched small business owners in Lahore struggle with the same problem: their WhatsApp and Instagram were flooded with messages, but there were never enough hours to respond. Leads slipped away. Customers got frustrated. Sales were lost — not because of bad products, but because of slow communication.

They built Sanestix in 2024 to solve exactly that. Not with generic chatbot tools that businesses have to bend themselves around — but with custom automation that learns your workflow, speaks in your voice, and handles the repetitive stuff so you and your team can focus on what only humans can do.
