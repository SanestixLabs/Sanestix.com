Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 7, 2025

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Sanestix collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our automation services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Business information (company name, website, social media handles)
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • Communication data (messages, support tickets, feedback)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our services, we automatically collect:

  • Usage data (features used, actions taken, time spent)
  • Device information (IP address, browser type, operating system)
  • Log data (access times, pages viewed, links clicked)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our automation services
  • Process transactions and send service-related communications
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Detect, prevent, and address security issues and fraud

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, access controls, and regular security audits to protect your information.
