Refund Policy

Last Updated: February 6, 2026

Our Commitment: At Sanestix, customer satisfaction is our priority. We're committed to providing high-quality automation services and fair refund policies.

1. Money-Back Guarantee

14-Day Satisfaction Guarantee

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for first-time subscribers. If you're not satisfied with our services within the first 14 days of your initial subscription, you can request a full refund.

Conditions for 14-Day Guarantee:

  • Valid only for new customers on their first subscription
  • Refund request must be submitted within 14 days of initial payment
  • You must have attempted to use the service and contacted support for assistance
  • Account must not have violated our Terms of Service
  • Does not apply to custom development or one-time setup fees

2. Refund Eligibility

Situations Eligible for Refunds:

  • Service Failure: If we fail to provide the core features promised and cannot resolve the issue within a reasonable timeframe
  • Technical Issues: Persistent technical problems that prevent you from using our services, despite support efforts
  • Billing Errors: Duplicate charges or incorrect billing amounts
  • Unauthorized Charges: Charges made without your authorization
  • Service Downtime: Extended service outages exceeding our SLA commitments (for Enterprise plans)

Situations NOT Eligible for Refunds:

  • Change of mind after the 14-day guarantee period
  • Lack of use or failure to activate the service
  • Account suspension or termination due to Terms of Service violations
  • Third-party platform restrictions (WhatsApp, Instagram, Shopify limitations)
  • Renewal charges when you failed to cancel before the renewal date
  • Custom development work that has been completed
  • One-time setup fees after setup has been completed

If approved, refunds are processed within 7-10 business days to your original payment method

4. Partial Refunds

In certain circumstances, we may offer partial refunds:

  • Pro-rated Refunds: For annual subscriptions cancelled mid-term due to qualifying reasons
  • Service Downgrades: Credit toward a lower-tier plan if downgrading mid-cycle
  • Unused Features: In cases where specific features were unavailable for extended periods

Partial refund amounts are determined on a case-by-case basis and at Sanestix's discretion.

5. Subscription Cancellations

How to Cancel

You can cancel your subscription at any time through:

  • Your account dashboard under "Billing & Subscription"
  • Contacting our support team

Cancellation Terms

  • Effective Date: Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period
  • Access: You retain access to services until the end of the paid period
  • No Refunds: Cancellations do not result in refunds for the remainder of the current billing period (except within 14-day guarantee)
  • Data Retention: Your data will be retained for 90 days after cancellation, then permanently deleted

6. Auto-Renewal and Billing

Important: All subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled before the renewal date.

  • You will receive a reminder email 7 days before renewal
  • Cancel at least 24 hours before renewal to avoid being charged
  • Refunds are not provided for auto-renewals you forgot to cancel

7. Chargebacks and Disputes

If you dispute a charge with your bank or payment provider without contacting us first:

  • Your account will be immediately suspended pending investigation
  • We may charge a $50 chargeback processing fee
  • Fraudulent chargebacks may result in permanent account termination

Please contact us first if you have billing concerns. We're committed to resolving issues fairly and quickly.

8. Custom Development and Setup Fees

One-Time Setup Fees

Setup fees are non-refundable once the setup process has been completed, including:

  • Initial system configuration
  • WhatsApp Business API setup
  • Integration with third-party platforms
  • Custom chatbot training

Custom Development

Custom development work follows a different refund policy:

  • Before Work Begins: Full refund available
  • Work in Progress: Refund of unused portion based on completion percentage
  • Work Completed: No refunds, but we'll work with you to make necessary revisions

9. Exceptions and Special Cases

Enterprise Customers

Enterprise subscription refund policies may differ based on your custom contract. Please refer to your Enterprise Agreement.

Annual Subscriptions

Annual subscriptions receive discounted pricing in exchange for a longer commitment:

  • 14-day money-back guarantee applies
  • After 14 days, pro-rated refunds may be considered for exceptional circumstances
  • Remaining balance may be offered as credit for future services

Promotional Offers and Discounts

Refunds for subscriptions purchased with promotional codes or discounts will be calculated based on the discounted amount paid, not the original price.

10. Force Majeure

We are not liable for refunds due to service interruptions caused by:

  • Natural disasters
  • War, terrorism, or civil unrest
  • Government actions or regulations
  • Internet service provider outages
  • Third-party platform outages (WhatsApp, Instagram, Shopify)

In such cases, we will make reasonable efforts to restore service and may offer service credits at our discretion.

11. Refund Methods

Refunds are processed using the same payment method used for the original purchase:

  • Credit/Debit Card: 7-10 business days to appear on your statement
  • PayPal: 5-7 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 10-14 business days

Processing times may vary depending on your financial institution. Sanestix is not responsible for delays caused by payment processors or banks.
