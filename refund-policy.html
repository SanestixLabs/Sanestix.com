Refund Policy

Last Updated: February 7, 2025

Customer Satisfaction Guaranteed. We want you to be happy with our services. This Refund Policy outlines the terms for requesting refunds and cancellations.

1. 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new customers. If you're not satisfied with our services within the first 14 days of your initial subscription, you can request a full refund.

1.1 Eligibility

  • First-time subscribers only
  • Request must be made within 14 days of initial purchase
  • Applicable to monthly and annual plans
  • One refund per customer

2. Subscription Cancellations

2.1 How to Cancel

You can cancel your subscription at any time through:

  • Your account dashboard settings
  • Email to support@sanestix.com
  • Contact our support team via WhatsApp

2.2 Cancellation Terms

  • Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing cycle
  • You'll continue to have access until the end of the paid period
  • No refunds for partial months or unused time after the 14-day guarantee period

3. Annual Plan Refunds

For annual subscriptions:

  • Full refund available within 14 days of purchase
  • After 14 days, no refunds for remaining months
  • You may cancel to prevent auto-renewal for the next year

4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Subscription fees after the 14-day guarantee period
  • Setup fees or one-time service fees
  • Add-on services or features
  • Accounts terminated for Terms of Service violations

5. Service Interruptions

If our services experience significant downtime (more than 24 consecutive hours), you may be eligible for a prorated credit or refund at our discretion.

6. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us with:

  1. Your account email address
  2. Reason for refund request
  3. Date of original purchase
  4. Any relevant details about your experience

Contact for Refunds:
Email: billing@sanestix.com
Subject Line: "Refund Request - [Your Email]"

7. Refund Processing

Approved refunds will be processed within:

  • 5-7 business days for credit/debit cards
  • 10-14 business days for bank transfers

Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for purchase.

8. Chargebacks

Please contact us before initiating a chargeback. Chargebacks may result in:

  • Immediate account suspension
  • Inability to use our services in the future
  • Additional fees to cover chargeback processing costs

9. Changes to Pricing

If we increase pricing, existing customers will:

  • Receive 30 days' advance notice
  • Be able to cancel before the increase takes effect
  • Keep current pricing for the remainder of their billing cycle

10. Contact Us

Questions about refunds or billing? We're here to help!
